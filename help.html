How to print and cut our sticker sheets:

Step One:

  • Ensure your Cricut critter is in good spirits by running a quick test. A happy Cricut is a crafty Cricut! If you need sticker paper here is what we recommend: Online Labels.
  • Set up your blade with gentle precision. Like a wise old fox, adjust the settings just right. Foxiary Studios suggests using this friendly sticker paper for picture-perfect prints.

Step Two:

  • Choose your favorite stickers from Foxiary Studios' delightful collection. Maybe a cute panda or a whimsical unicorn? The animal kingdom is your crafting oyster!
  • Upload the chosen design to Cricut Design Space. Watch as each unique creature prepares to join your crafting escapade.

Step Three:

  • Open Cricut Design Space and set the stage on a cozy 6x9 mat. Make it a welcoming space for your stickers to frolic!
  • Press that magical cut button, and witness the charming symphony of creatures coming to life.
  • Your stickers are now ready to add a touch of whimsy to the world!
