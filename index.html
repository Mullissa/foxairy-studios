About Me

Hello!

My name is Mullissa Caroline and I am a digital artist, UX/UI Designer, and frontend developer. I currently live in Denver and I have two bunnies. I enjoy visual design and color so, naturally, I enjoy drawing, painting, and graphic design. My other passions are psychology and business. It is so interesting to me to see how emotion plays such a huge role in our decisions and then also the business side of things in regards to marketing and strategy play a role in the success of branding for a business.
I love to create cute and colorful stickers for your planner. I hope you enjoy my stickers as much as I enjoy making them!
